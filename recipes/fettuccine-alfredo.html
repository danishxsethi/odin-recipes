Fettuccine-Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Description

A rich and classic Italian pasta dish, featuring thick fettuccine noodles coated in a luxurious, creamy sauce made simply from unsalted butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredents

  • 1 lb Fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted butter
  • 1 cup Heavy cream (or whipping cream)
  • 1 1/2 cups Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove Garlic (minced, optional)
  • Salt, fresh black pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta: Boil water and cook the fettuccine al dente according to package directions. Reserve 1 cup of the starchy pasta water before draining.
  2. Make Sauce Base: In a wide pan, melt the butter over low heat. Add garlic (if using) and cook for 1 minute. Add the heavy cream and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Emulsify: Remove the pan from the heat. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and whisk quickly and constantly until the cheese melts and the sauce is smooth.
  4. Toss: Add the drained fettuccine to the sauce. Toss immediately to coat evenly. Add reserved pasta water 1 tablespoon at a time if the sauce is too thick.
  5. Finish: Season with salt, black pepper, and a tiny pinch of nutmeg. Serve immediately.
