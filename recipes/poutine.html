Poutine

Description

A beloved Canadian classic hailing from Quebec. This ultimate comfort food consists of crispy french fries topped with fresh cheese curds and smothered in hot, savory brown gravy.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Russet potatoes (cut into sticks)
  • 2 cups Cheese curds (fresh and squeaky)
  • 2 cups Beef broth
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp All-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Fries: Soak the cut potatoes in cold water for 1 hour to remove starch. Drain and pat dry completely.

  3. Make the Gravy: In a saucepan, melt butter and whisk in flour to make a roux. Slowly add beef broth, whisking constantly until thickened. Season with salt and pepper. Keep warm.
  4. Second Fry (Crisping): Increase oil heat to 375째F (190째C). Fry the potatoes again for 2-4 minutes until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Assemble: Place hot fries in a bowl. Sprinkle cheese curds generously on top.
  6. Serve: Pour the hot gravy over the fries and curds immediately so the cheese slightly melts. Eat with a fork!
